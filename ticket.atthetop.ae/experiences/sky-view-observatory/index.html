About Sky Views Observatory

Located on Levels 52 and 53 of the Address Sky View Hotel, Sky Views Observatory is an unforgettable place 53 floors up, 219 meters high above.

Enjoy the dazzling Dubai skyline, walk on a glass floor with the cityscape right beneath your feet, or slide down a glass slide for a thrilling ride, and if you’re into adrenaline pumping experiences you can also add on The Edge Walk adventure.

Useful Tips

Know before you go

The Sky Views Observatory is located on Levels 52 and 53 of the Address Sky View Hotel and is a one-of-a-kind experience filled with adventure, thrills, and adrenaline-pumping activities.

  • Getting there: Take the Dubai Metro to the Burj Khalifa/Dubai Mall station, and then walk to Sky Views Observatory.By car use GPS to navigate to The Address Sky View Hotel, Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Boulevard, Downtown Dubai.
  • Entrance location: Address Sky View Hotel, Tower 1.
  • Nearest parking: Address Sky View Hotel, Tower 1.
  • Food & beverage: Outside food and beverage is not allowed , there’s a causal dining restaurant that serves international cuisine within Sky Views Observatory.
  • Items to be left at the counter prior to your visit:
    Large bags, strollers, electronics, and sharp items must be left at the dedicated counter.
  • Dress Code: We kindly ask that you wear casual, comfortable clothing. Please refrain from wearing high heels, flip flops, or loose footwear.
  • Souvenir Shops: Discover a curated collection of souvenirs perfect for a cherished memento or a gift for your loved ones.
